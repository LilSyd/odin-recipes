Tasty Avocado Toast

This is a very easy and yummy avocado toast recipe to start the day with lots of energy. It takes a bit more time in the kitchen than a bowl of cereal with milk but it'll keep you full way longer!

In case you're not sure why you'd read this recipe of avocado toast, let me tell you that since I've made it for my partner and I one day, he's been asking me to make it every other day since!

Ingredients (for 2 people)

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices of toast (any kind)
  • Lemon juice (can be replaced by anything else that's acidic)
  • Salt, pepper, paprika, chiliflakes (almost any spice)
  • Optional: fried eggs

Steps

  1. Toast your slices until golden brown, let it cool before putting on the plates (keeps the bread crispy).
  2. In the meantime, slice your avocado in half and remove the pit.
  3. Cute dices into the avocado, then use a fork to mash a bit before transfering to the toast.
  4. Spread the avocado on the toast, covering the entire surface of the toast.
  5. Add lemon juice to the toast, then salf, pepper and any other spice you like.
  6. Stop here and you'll have a delicious avocado toast to enjoy. Or fry up and egg and put in on top for extra protein.

This should get you full enough to last until the next meal! (And if not, you can always grab a snack *wink*.)
